About This ETF Universe

All ETFs in this list are pre-screened using these criteria:

  • Asset class: Equities, Fixed Income, or Commodities only
  • Holdings region: North America
  • AUM: Over $1 billion
  • Management style: Passive only
  • Leverage: Non-leveraged
  • Expense ratio: Under 0.5%

This creates a list of ETFs that are i) liquid with low management fees and tracking error, and ii) pure-play and passively managed funds whose underperformance, if any, is likely due to market cycles rather than poor fund management.

Note: For leveraged ETFs, search their unleveraged version (e.g., QQQ instead of TQQQ).
